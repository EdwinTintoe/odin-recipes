Red lentil Dhal

Description

Delicious vegan red lentil Dhal to warm you up in the upcoming winter.

Feel free to add vegetables such as carrots, cauliflower, potatoes etc.

Ingredients

  • Washed split red lentils
  • Coconut oil
  • Mustard seed
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger root
  • Chilli
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Ground cumin
  • Ground coriander
  • Ground aniseed
  • Turmeric powder
  • Ground cloves
  • Vegetable stock
  • Coconut milk
  • Salt
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Heat up coconut oil in a pot.
  2. Add mustard seeds and wait for them to start popping.
  3. Add chopped onion, ginger root, garlic and chilli, and sautee until caramelised.
  4. Add ground spices and fry until aroma is released.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes to the mix, and cook together to make a masala.
  6. Stir in washed split red lentils (may also be brown, green, black).
  7. Top up with vegetable stock, reduce to simmer, and cook until lentils are done. Add salt to taste.
  8. Add coconut milk towards the very end.
  9. Finish off with a squeeze of lime and some fresh coriander if you have it.

Bon apetit!
