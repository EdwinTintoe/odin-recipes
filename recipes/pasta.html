Pasta with tomato sauce

Description

A classic pasta dish with traditional tomato sauce. Simple but excellent.

You can use the sauce as a base for other pasta dishes, as well as a pizza sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Chopped tomatoes or roasted tomatoes if you're feeling fancy
  • Chicken or vegetable stock
  • Basil (can be rosemary or thyme)
  • Salt
  • White pepper
  • Mozarella

Steps

  1. Heat up olive oil in a sauce pan.
  2. Add chopped onion, carrot, garlic, and celery, and saute until caramelised.
  3. Add tomatoes to the pot. Chopped tomatoes are fine, but nothing beats fresh tomatoes, which have been halved and oven-roasted with basil or rosemary.
  4. Add stock, herbs and seasoning, and simmer until carrots are soft. You might need to top up the stock as it evaporates in the process to get the right consistency.
  5. In another pot, bring water and salt to boil, and cook your favourite pasta al dente.
  6. When the carrots are cooked, blend the sauce to a smooth consistency with a blender.
  7. Strain the pasta but leave a bit of water in, and throw it in the sauce.
  8. Coat the pasta fully with sauce, add mozarella, and serve with fresh basil leaf.

Bon apetit!
