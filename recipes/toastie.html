Cheese, onion and mushroom Toastie

Description

A quick and simple home classic.

Ideal when you can't be bothered - like I can't with this last recipe.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Mushroom
  • Onion
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat your toastie machine.
  2. Lay the bread with sliced cheese, onion, and mushroom into a sandwich
  3. Butter one side of the sandwich, and place into your machine buttered side down.
  4. Butter the top side. This way your hands are not greasy.
  5. Let the technology do the rest for you.

Enjoy that stringy cheese ya numpty!
